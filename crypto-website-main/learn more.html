About Sovrin

Sovrin is a sleek and powerful cryptocurrency tracker designed to provide users with real-time information about various cryptocurrencies. Made by Shreyas Kumar, Rishabh Mishra, and Rudransh Bajpai as founders and many more contributors.

Features

  • Real-time cryptocurrency prices: Get updated prices of various cryptocurrencies in real-time.
  • Price change tracking: Track price changes over time to make informed decisions.
  • Customizable watchlist: Create a personalized watchlist of your favorite cryptocurrencies.
  • Portfolio tracking: Monitor your cryptocurrency portfolio's performance with ease.
  • User-friendly interface: Easy-to-use interface for a seamless experience.
